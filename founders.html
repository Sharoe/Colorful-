Welcome to Founders High School

About Founders High School

Founders High School was established at Herbert Stanley Hostel, Barham Green, Bulawayo in January 1952. It Strives by a motto "Truth is Ever Simple.

Vision

To prepare boys and girls to adopt an all encompassing approach to adult life through progressive formal and informal educational training programs.

Mission Statement

We seek to offer quality education through a broad balanced and relevant school curriculum that is responsive to the needs of our environment Integrated learning experiences, and appropriate individual tuition programmes in order o develop a child who is able to think rotationally and critically each according to his/her ability.

To achieve this they will:

  1. Impart academic tuition and technological skills relevant to the needs of our environments
  2. Avail equal opportunity to all aspects of the curriculum irrespective of disability, sex, race and culture
  3. Prepare the individual child for the opportunities, responsibilities and experiences of adult life.
  4. Provide each individual with a stimulating, challenging and supportive learning environment.
  5. Endeavour to develop a disciplined student who is physically and mentally fit by his/her full participation in core curriculum.
  6. Seek to produce a whole person who is resourceful, creative and interactive, a person who can adapt to any society with a sense of personal worth and confidence, a person who has an awareness of political, social, spiritual, cultural and aesthetic norms and values.

A view of the school.

Values

Dedication, Integrity, Transparency, Commitment, Open-mindedness, Excellence, Passion, Co-operation,Honesty, Self-reliance, Respect and Mutual Respect

Academics at Founders High School

Commerce, Accounts, Literature in English, English Language, Ndebele, Music, Art, Fashion and Fabrics, Food and Nutrition, Theatre Arts, Computer Science, Woodwork and Metalwork.

Advanced Level Sujects

Chemistry, Business Studies, Physics, Biology, Agriculture, Food Science, Sociology, Ndebele, Literature, History, Geography, Accounts and Economics.

Fees Structure

The termly school fees is $105 for scholars excluding a practical fee of $15.
