Welcome to Parklands High School

At Parklands High School we pride ourselves on what we can offer our pupils. We offer a rich diversity of academic, cultural and sport activities to develop our students to their full potential. We see our school as a pacesetter for other schools and have a highly trained and professional staff who are fully committed to what they do. We are a family and strive only for the best, be it for our students, parents, staff or the communities whom we serve.

Parklands High School Mission Statement

Parklands High School is a caring and collaborative learning community that embraces Zimbabwe's diversity while blazing a trail in Service, Academia, Sport and Culture. Our school ensures that learners achieve intellectual and personal excellence and are well prepared for a global community as responsible, productive and engaged citizens.

Academics at Parklands High School

Academic achievement at Parklands High School has been excellent over the past years. We have a great team of highly trained and professional staff who are fully committed to provide the best educational experience possible. We are focused on regular and rigorous work and continue to find ways to encourage pupils to maintain a good work ethic and work with dedication and pride. We are committed to teach at a standard and level of difficulty that stretches our pupils academically beyond the scope of the formal syllabus and our pupils are encouraged to ensure they achieve results that reflect their abilities. At our annual Academic Awards Assemblies and Celebrations, achievers are recognized for their exceptional work. We also have our Academic Support Programme which is compulsory for persistent defaulters who do not reach their potential. We, the staff, pupils and Governors at PHS are committed to each pupil reaching their full potential. Over the past couple of years we have achieved a 100% pass rate.

Parklands High School Fees Structure

In accordance with a decision made by the School Governing Body at Parklands High School, school fees for the year of 2017 have now been finalised. HOW MUCH DO YOU PAY ? The school fees are now set at $3 750 for the year of 2017.(this figure excludes books, uniforms, camps and outings)

School fees are paid in advance and are due on the FIRST DAY OF SCHOOL. Fees are payable either in full, or in 10 monthly EQUAL instalments from 31 January to 31 October. If parents are in arrears with ONE instalment, then the FULL amount becomes due and payable immediately.

DO YOU QUALIFY FOR A DISCOUNT ? If you pay in full before 31 January 2017, the school fees are $2 900, which represents a saving of $175 and you will be paying the same fees as you paid in 2016.
